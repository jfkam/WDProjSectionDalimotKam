What is mental health ?
Mental health is the state of complete physical, mental, social well being and free from any disease or infirmity of a person, as defined by the World Health Organization (WHO). In other words, it is the overall physiological welfare and condition of one. It is built on top of many factors —family, environment, education, and influences.
Signs of mental illness
Mental illness can show up in many forms often in extreme mood swings, poor appetite, sleeping patterns, behavioral changes and withdrawal of social involvement. These are just a few of the symptoms shown at the early stages of mental illnesses.
Forms of mental illness
Mental illness come in numerous different forms, but they commonly show up as anxiety disorders, depression, bipolar, PTSD, phobias, and any disruptive disorder.
Possible factors/causes
Mental illness can be a factor of a combination of biological (e.g. genetics), psychological (e.g. trauma), and environmental — the surrounding influences. They all play a role that could possibly trigger a disorder, changing the course of one’s life.

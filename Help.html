Need Help?

Emphasis on Suicide

Always remember that suicide will not solve the problem, you’re departing away from your loved ones — the people who’ve stayed by your side in every moments of your life. If you need help, reach out to your local support.

Simple Ways to Help

Find something that can bring motivation or “light” back to your life. It does not require extreme expenses but simple things such as strolling in the park – which is free for everyone. You can even seek guidance from a trusted friend/adult.
